Budget Ledger Lite Android

A local-first personal finance tracker that keeps your money — and your data — exactly where it belongs: on your device.

Google Play 📄 Privacy Policy 📄 Terms of Service
🔒

No Signup Required

Zero friction — install and start logging. No email, no account, no password reset emails ever.

📱

Stays on Your Device

All data lives locally. No server, no cloud sync, no one else can see your financial life.

📅

Calendar View

Tap any day on the interactive month grid to view transactions and add new ones at a glance.

💼

Multiple Accounts

Track Personal, Business, Savings, or custom accounts — each color-coded and separated cleanly.

🔁

Recurring Transactions

Set up bills and income on daily, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, or yearly schedules. Edit one or all instances.

🔍

Smart Search

Full-text search across all your transactions and accounts instantly — no loading, no server calls.

🔔

Balance Alerts

Get a push notification the moment an account balance drops below your custom threshold.

🤞

App Lock

Protect your data with a PIN or biometric (fingerprint / face unlock) with a configurable timeout.

📊

Monthly Summary

At-a-glance income, expenses, and net balance per account — know exactly where you stand each month.

💾

Backup & Restore

Export your data as JSON anytime, and restore it just as easily. Optional daily auto-backup included.

🌑

Dark & Light Themes

Follows your system preference automatically, or set it manually. Five accent colors to personalize.

🆓

Free to Use

Fully featured at no cost. Supported by a small banner ad — no paywalls, no feature locks, ever.
